Name: Haji Sayed Mohammad Daud Nasiri

Previous Functions:

1.     Elected senator of daikundi province.

2.     Worked as adviser for IRD in daikundi province.

3.     Worked as adviser in peace council process with ISAF.

4.     He worked as senior elders rendered social service to daikundi people.

Biodata:

Haji Sayed Daud Nasiri, son of Haji Ayub Shah Kijran, was born in 1957 in the village of kando in kojran district of Daikundi province. He completed 12 standard classes and further he has bachelor in law and politics from Bakhtar University Kabul.

He was elected senator of Daikundi Province to the Mesharno Jirga (House of People) of Afghanistan in 2005 – 2010.

After Election he success to win the election to become member of house of people.
