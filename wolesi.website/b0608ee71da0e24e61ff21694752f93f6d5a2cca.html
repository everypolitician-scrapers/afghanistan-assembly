Tuesday 07 August 2018


Haji Nisar Ahmad Faizi Ghoryani

Province: Herat

District: Ghuryan

Votes: 17906

Phone: 0799694481

Age: 50

New Member

First language: Dari

Party Affiliation: Independent

Commission (2012): Defense and Territorial Affairs

Personal background: Haji Nisar Ahmad Faizi Ghoryani was born in 1967 in Ghuryan district, Herat province. He is married and has one son and two daughters. He speaks Dari, English and Pashto.

Education: Ghoryani graduated from Ghulam Haidar Khan high school in 1982. BA in Business Administration.

Previous occupation: Ghoryani is a former Mujahideen commander. He joined the Mujahideen forces in Herat in 1987 and became an advisor to Ismail Khan. He later immigrated to the United States after his injury. Ghoryani is also a well-respected businessman, humanitarian and philanthropist in Afghanistan. He is the owner of the Liberty Corporation Group of companies in Herat, Afghanistan.
