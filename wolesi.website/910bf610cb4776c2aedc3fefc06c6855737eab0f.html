BIO DATA OF HAJI OBAIDULLAH BARAKZAI

Haji Obaidullah Barakzai is a prominent Afghan, an influential Politician, and a current Member of the Lower house and the Chairman of the Commission of Complaints, Petitions, of the Lower House of Afghanistan.

Early Life

·      Haji Obaidullah Barakzai was born in 1959 in a wealthy Pashtun family in Afghanistan. He belongs to the Barakzai Pashtun tribe. He spent his childhood in the Chora District, Urozgan Province.

·      Mr. Barakzai completed his primary and secondary education at Chora District in Urozgan Province. He also studied Islamic theology.

·      In 2016, he earned his Bachelor’s Degree in Law and Political Science from the Bakhatar University in Kabul.

·      Mr. Barakzai is a man of tremendous intellect, talent, capacity and he is gifted with a cheerful personality. He is extremely articulate in speech and expression and bears a persuasive and mature personality. He is emotionally stable and intellectually capable.

Political Career

·      In 1982, Barakzai joined the Hezb-e-Islami Party of Afghanistan. He served as the Commander for the Mujahideen, who at the time were fighting to expel Soviet Army troops during the Soviet war in Afghanistan (1982–1989). 

·      After the downfall of the Taliban in late 2001, he served the Military Commander in Kandahar Airport for 6 years and played an important role in maintaining peace and security.

·      On 2006, he served as the District-Governor of Khas Urozgan in Urozgan province. For the next four years, he advocated people‘s rights and worked for local governance. 

·      On 2006, Barakzai joined office in Urozgan as the Director of the Ministry of Frontiers and Tribal affairs of Afghanistan and he served till 2010. During his term, he admitted hundreds of young students to Rahman Baba and Khushal khan High Schools. He worked hard to educate several young children to save their lives from dark and hard times.

·      On 2010, he registered as a candidate for the 2010 Afghan Lower House election. His campaign focused on Reconstruction of schools, universities, roads and bridges, the eradication of poverty, employment opportunities for citizens under a dynamic economy, raising standards of living, providing scholarships opportunities, strengthening police capacities and maintaining peace and security in Urozgan.

·      On 2010, after the audit results, he won 2251 of the popular votes and was subsequently declared as the Member of Lower House by the Independent Election Commission.

·      At present he holds the dual capacity as a Member of the Lower house and as the Chairman of Commission of Complaints, Petitions of Lower House in Afghanistan’s Parliament.

·      Barakzai has attended to Iran as an official delegate of the Lower House with related protections of Refuges and internally displaced people who seek asylum, and Turkey with regard to Contemporary Situations of Afghanistan.

·      Barkzai has attended various ceremonies, Graduations, national conferences and he has also involved himself in burning political discussions in the press and media 

·      His experience has provided him with an intimate understanding of all political and complicated issues in Afghanistan. He has shown the ability to clearly articulate a well analysed argument and present them well to his audience. A clear grasp of reality and the ability to reason logically seem to come naturally to him.

Achievements

  He has been a Member and Chairman of the commission on Complaints Petitions, MP’s privileges and Immunities for seven consecutive years and has played an important role in solving complicated litigations, both judicial and extra Judicial. He has carried out extensive reforms, with regard to transparency and accountability between government and citizens.

During his term as the parliament’s Commission on complaints and Petitions he played a key role in the prevention of nepotism and corruption by government officials and warlord-like local commanders and doctorial orders. His reform policies focused on poverty eradication and the establishment of citizenship rights, transparency, and protections of citizen’s right against Government officials.

He has always directed special attention towards the education of the youth; he has provided more than 100 scholarships to Urozgan students who are pursuing their education in Egypt, Turkey and India.

His dedication, punctuality and hardworking nature keep him well ahead of his peers. He also maintains a very strong rapport with his peers and is a team player. All the other  Members of the Lower house and his fellow Politicians hold him in high regard.

Personal Life

Barakzai is married and has twenty five children. At presently lives in Kabul, Afghanistan

Barakzai has lived a bitter life during which he has lost 28 members of his family during the civil war in Afghanistan.

Barakzai experienced the death of his elder son at the hands of the Taliban in Kandahar while he was returning from the Mosque. And was also deprived of his young educated son who was martyred.(Shahid) Hafizullah Barakzai, graduated BA. (Political Science) and Master’s Degree in MA.( Political Science) Chandigarh University, Punjab, India. Mr. Haji Hafizullah was shot down by Terrorists while he attempted to save the lives of innocent people in Kabul.  Mr. Haji Hafizullah was very bright and intelligent and had excellent prospects, with a strong desire to complete his PhD and serve his People. Unfortunately, he has moved on toward a perpetual journey.
