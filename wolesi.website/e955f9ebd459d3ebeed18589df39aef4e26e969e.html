Obaidullah Ramin personal life

Obaidullah Ramin son of Mohammad Omar was born in 1952 at Frenk district of Baghlan province.

Current add: Kabul

Education: Bachelor in economy and agriculture expansion

Marital state: married

Electoral district: Baghlan province
