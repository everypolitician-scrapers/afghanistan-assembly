Fariba Ahmadi Kakar’s biography

Province: Kandahar

District: 1st District

Votes: 2289

Phone: 0799274139

Age: 37 Incumbent

First language: Pashto

Party Affiliation: Independent

Commission (2012): Transport, Telecommunications, Urban and Housing Affairs, Water and Power Supply and Municipal Affairs

Personal background: Fariba Ahmadi Kakar was born in 1974 in the first district of Kandahar city, Kandahar province. She is married with three daughters and one son. She has traveled to Canada. Her interests include reading and writing. She speaks Pashto and Dari.

Education: Kakar graduated from the Aeno Girls’ High School in Kandahar in 1983.

Previous occupation: Kakar was head of the women’s department of the Afghan Development Association (ADA) from 2000-2005. She then taught for the Afghan Women’s Association for two months.

2005 Parliament: Kakar was elected to the 2005 parliament with 2,930 votes. She served on the Transport, Telecommunications, Urban and Housing Affairs, Water and Power Supply and Municipal Affairs commission during this term.
