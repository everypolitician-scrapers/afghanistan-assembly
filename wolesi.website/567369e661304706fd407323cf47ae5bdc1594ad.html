Haji Abdul Zahair Qadir Personal life

Haji Abdul Zahair Qadir son of Haji Qadir Khan was born in 1358, his father Haji Qadir worked for different government’s key posts like a first secretary president, minister for public works and even  governor of   Nangarhar province .

Haji Abul Zahair Qadir has finished his primary and high education in Pakistan and after that he went Arabia for learning and improving his religious education.

During the Dr Najebullah’s regime Haji Zahair Qadir was refugee out the country, but after the collapse of communism's regime he came back to Afghanistan.

Haji Abdul  Zahair Qadir was prisoner during the Taliban regime for a while in Kandahar province and has  unforgettable  memories from jail as he says,  those  Jihadi commander’s  who   was arrested  had been punished by Taliban  in jail.

Haji Abdul Zahair Qadir even fights against Taliban, so he was as a good commander for controlling of security situation to ensure peace and stability in the country.

Haji Abdul Zahair Qadir was selected as a  representative  at the second round of parliament and after  a short time he selected as first secretary in  parliament by  obtaining of  140 positive vote  and beside that  he create a   peace  Carwan group in parliament, so he is now as national person in the country.

Haji Abul Zahair Qadir built a educational institute that has capacity of thousand students and beside that he built a eye hospital in Nangarhar province.
